# App required data from tmdb api

## Movie

* Title
* Year
* Synopsis
* Genres
* Studio
* Posters
* Credits


## People

* Name
* Date Of Birth
* Bio
* Images
* Credits

## Credits

Since a person may occupy multiple roles in a movie, both on the cast and crew, I want to list all the roles together by actor or by movie.

### Movie Credits Structure

| Person | Roles |
|--------|-------|
| Leonardo DiCaprio | "Cobb", Producer |

### People Credits Structure

| Movie | Year | Roles |
|-------|------|-------|
| Inception | 2010 | "Cobb", Producer |
